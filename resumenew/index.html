Welcome!

Hello and welcome to my resume webpage.

My name is Seth Templeton, I'm 20 years old and currently working on my Associates in Arts degree for digital media, in the hopes that I can someday work in the video game design scene. Designing and modeling truly are my passion, and it is in my utmost of interest to make it my job. My hopes are to someday put all the ideas I have to use, and I see the video game format as the best way to do that, due to their interactive quality. My aspiration is something like Masahiro Ito, designing so many memorable characters and settings, I like to think these ideas of mine have potential, I just need to get better at executing them. I've practiced my ideas through several mediums, to which I find sketching and then 3d modeling my prefered method of expressing my ideas.
